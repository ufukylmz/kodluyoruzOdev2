Profiteroles Recipe

Introduction

Welcome to our delicious profiteroles recipe! In this guide, we'll show you how to make these delightful French pastries filled with cream and topped with chocolate sauce. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate sauce

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine water and butter, and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add the flour and stir vigorously until the mixture forms a ball.
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
  4. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition until the dough is smooth.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Drop spoonfuls of the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the profiteroles are golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and let them cool completely.
  9. Cut a small slit in each profiterole and fill with whipped cream.
  10. Drizzle chocolate sauce over the profiteroles and serve.

Conclusion

That's it! You've successfully made delicious profiteroles. Enjoy these light and airy pastries filled with creamy goodness. They make a perfect dessert for any occasion.
